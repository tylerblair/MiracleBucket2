-- Who Are We --

Who is the Miracle Bucket Society?

The Miracle Bucket Society is a local, registered and incorporated “non-profit society” which began in the North Okanagan in 2011. We have a vision to make a difference for families experiencing socioeconomic and/or health challenges in our community. These are families who fall between the cracks and are not able to get assistance through other resources.

The society is about promoting the beliefs that:

Living is giving and believing in miracles.

When we start to do what is necessary, then do what is possible, suddenly we have communities doing the impossible.

Giving anonymously is a random act of kindness that provides a hand up not a hand out.

Funded activities will provide direct service to the community and take an innovative approach to community concerns.

Why become a part of the Society?

It is a way to give back to our community.

It is anonymous. A simple and an effective way to pay it forward.

This is an option for giving that goes directly to a family – no administration costs or salaries.

How does it work?

Referrals are made to the Miracle Bucket Society by designated representatives from various local registered charities and/or health care professionals within the North Okanagan region. Miracle gifts are given anonymously to eligible families in the form of gift cards for Real Canadian Superstore and it’s affiliates to purchase food, clothing and household items such as bedding, towels, pillows, dishes, pots etc. Criteria….A family is defined as an adult and his/her child (or children) living in the same household. Priority is given to families living in Vernon, Coldstream, Lumby, Cherryville and/or Armstrong. Referrals for consideration by The Miracle Bucket Society come through the following organizations and/or local registered charities in good standing and who demonstrate fiscal responsibility.

•Interior Health Authority:Emergency,Palliative Care, Pediatric and Mental Health

•Canadian Mental Health Association

•Vernon Woman’s Transition House
